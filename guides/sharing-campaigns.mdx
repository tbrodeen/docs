## Your campaign URL

Every campaign gets a public URL at:

```
backvote.co/c/your-slug
```

Supporters who visit this link can immediately record and submit an endorsement — no account or login required.

## Sharing channels

The campaign share step (and the campaign detail page) provides one-click sharing to:

| Channel | How it works |
|---------|-------------|
| **Direct link** | Copy the URL to share anywhere |
| **QR code** | Download a QR code image for print materials, event signage, or mailers |
| **SMS** | Opens a pre-filled text message with your campaign link |
| **Email** | Opens a pre-filled email with your campaign link and a short intro |
| **Facebook** | Share directly to Facebook |
| **Instagram** | Copy link optimized for Instagram bio or stories |
| **WhatsApp** | Share via WhatsApp message |

## Best practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Events and town halls">
    Display the QR code on screens or printed signage at events. Supporters can scan and record endorsements on the spot while the energy is high.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email blasts">
    Include the campaign link in your email newsletters with a clear call-to-action. Mention it takes under 60 seconds to record.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Social media">
    Post your campaign link across social channels. Pin it to your profile or include it in your bio. Pair it with an existing supporter video to show people what to expect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Text/SMS campaigns">
    Text messages have high open rates. Send a short, personal message with the campaign link to your supporter list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Canvassing and door-knocking">
    Print the QR code on palm cards or leave-behinds so supporters can record at their convenience after the conversation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
