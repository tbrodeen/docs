## Overview

The video studio lets you polish supporter video submissions before publishing them in showcases. Access it from any video submission's detail view by clicking **Open in Studio**.

## Video player

The main panel displays the full video with playback controls. Videos are streamed via Cloudflare for fast, reliable playback at any resolution.

## Transcript panel

Every video submission gets an AI-generated transcript displayed alongside the video. The transcript is:

- **Time-synced** — follows along with video playback
- **Searchable** — find specific moments or quotes quickly
- **Useful for accessibility** — helps you identify key soundbites without watching the entire recording

## Editing tools

The studio provides auto-edit capabilities to quickly produce polished clips:

- **Trim** — cut the beginning or end to remove dead air or fumbled starts
- **Brand overlay** — add your campaign logo or name as a watermark
- **Auto-edit** — let the system suggest cuts based on transcript analysis

## Edit history

Every edit you make is tracked in the edit history panel. You can see what was changed, when, and by whom — and revert to any previous version if needed.

<Note>The original submission is always preserved. Edits create new versions rather than modifying the source recording.</Note>
