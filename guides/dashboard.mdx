## Overview

The dashboard is the first thing you see after logging in. It gives you a real-time snapshot of your endorsement collection efforts across all campaigns.

## Stats at a glance

Four stat cards sit at the top of your dashboard:

| Stat | What it tracks |
|------|---------------|
| **Active Campaigns** | Number of published campaigns currently accepting submissions |
| **Total Submissions** | Combined count of all endorsements received across campaigns |
| **Video Submissions** | Number of video endorsements specifically |
| **Showcase Views** | Total views across all your published showcases |

## Onboarding checklist

When you first create your account, the dashboard displays a guided onboarding hero with three steps to get you started:

1. Create your first campaign
2. Collect your first submission
3. Publish your first showcase

This checklist disappears once you've completed all three steps.

## Recent submissions

A table at the bottom of the dashboard shows your most recent submissions with supporter name, submission type, originating campaign, and timestamp. Click any row to jump directly to that submission's detail view.

## Analytics

Below the stat cards, charts visualize your engagement trends:

- **Submissions over time** — a line chart showing weekly submission volume
- **Breakdown by type** — distribution across video, voice, and text formats
