## Accessing settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar to open the settings hub. Settings are organized into several sections.

## Account

Manage your personal profile and organization details:

- **Name and avatar** — your display name and profile picture
- **Organization name** — the name of your campaign or organization
- **Organization logo** — displayed across your account

## Team

Invite team members to collaborate on your campaigns and submissions:

- Add members by email
- Assign roles and permissions
- Remove team members

## Billing

Manage your subscription and payment details:

- View your current plan
- Upgrade or downgrade
- Update payment method
- View billing history

## Campaign terms

Configure the terms of service text that supporters see when submitting endorsements. This text appears with a checkbox that supporters must accept before submitting.

Write your terms in the text editor and save. The terms apply to all campaigns that have the "Terms acceptance" setting enabled.

## Landing page terms

If you use custom landing pages (built with the campaign wizard's landing page step), you can configure separate terms for those pages.

## Moderation

Set your content moderation policies:

- Configure AI moderation sensitivity
- Define custom moderation rules
- Set default behavior for flagged content

## Privacy

Manage data handling and privacy settings for your organization and the supporters who submit endorsements.

## Support

Access help resources and contact the Back the Vote support team.
