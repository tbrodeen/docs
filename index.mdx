## What is Back the Vote?

Back the Vote is a platform that helps political campaigns, community organizations, and advocacy groups collect authentic endorsements from supporters. Your supporters record video, voice, or text endorsements directly from their browser — no app download required — and you curate and publish them in embeddable showcases.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a campaign">
    Use the 6-step campaign wizard to set up your endorsement collection page with custom branding, questions, and settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with supporters">
    Send your campaign link via SMS, email, or social media. Supporters record endorsements directly in their browser — no login needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and moderate">
    Incoming submissions land in your inbox. Approve, reject, or edit them with the built-in video studio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish showcases">
    Build embeddable galleries from your approved endorsements and display them on your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>
